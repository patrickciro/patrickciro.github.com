about me...

I'm a SAG-AFTRA, AEA actor based in New York City. My recent credits include the lead role in Deliverance (an independent film shot in New York City) and a role in Arthur Kopit's Chamber Music (directed by Austin Pendleton at the Abingdon Theater in New York City). I recently completed my first improv class at the Upright Citizen's Brigade.

my film work...

I played the lead role in Deliverance, a character-driven film about a Catholic priest struggling with alcoholism, guilt and depression after the death of his niece.
