about me...

I'm a SAG-AFTRA, AEA actor based in New York. Recent credits include the lead role in Deliverence (an independent film shot in New York City) and a role in Arthur Kopit's Chamber Music (directed by Austin Pendleton at the Abingdon Theater in New York City). I recently completed my first improv class at the Upright Citizen's Brigade.
