about me...

I'm a SAG-AFTRA/AEA actor based in New York City. My recent credits include the lead role in Deliverance (an independent short film shot in New York City) and a role in Arthur Kopit's Chamber Music (directed by Austin Pendleton at the Abingdon Theater in New York City).

I recently completed an improv class at the Upright Citizens Brigade. I also take figurative drawing and oil painting classes at the Art Students League of New York.
